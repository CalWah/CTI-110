Y2K Music

Y2k takes place around the late 90's and the early 2000's.

My interpretation of Y2k Music is pop music,electronic music, and some punk rock music.

One of the best examples of this are the artist Aqua.They make pop music and thier music is best shown is Barbie and My Oh My.

Another great example is the song Simple and Clean from Hikaru Utada. This song is also from the game Kingdom Hearts on the PS2, with is aslo part of my best Y2K games.

    Music Videos

    When in comes to the Y2K aesthetic, I think that music videos are very inportant to understand the time.

    Y2K music videos have over saturated colors, lens flare, fisheye lense, and futuristic and glossy artstyle. In a 4 by 3 aspect ratio.
